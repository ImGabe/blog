About

About me

I'm Gabe, a computer cience student at UERJ. Study on Rust, Go and functional programming.

Here is my github and you can also contact me via email.

Why?

Ever since I wrote my first line of code, I felt I should share my suffering experiences, either to help someone else who wants to venture down the same path or just to record my idiocies. All my not-so-usual experiences with any technology that looks interesting or anything else that I think needs will be documented.

How?

This blog is made with Zola and Nix and is hosted on my github. I chose to use Zola because of the simplicity and ease that this static site engine offers, besides the fact that it makes my daily routine even more rusty. Nix has been part of my routine for a few years now, however, flake is a great novelty that has been pleasing me a lot for the practicality of setting up a development environment.
